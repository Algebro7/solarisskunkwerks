As one of the dreaded Moirae, Atropos is the third sister, who cuts the Thread of Life. Truly the 'Mech is aptly named; a dedicated special operations platform designed not for heavy combat but rather to not be seen, a flanker and harrasser able to perform the coup de grace. This is not to imply that the 'Mech is lightly armed. A single prototype medium X-Pulse Laser of Federated Suns manufacture is mounted in each arm to supplement her main weapon, an MML-7 with two tons of ammunition. Gracing each shoulder of Atropos' frame is an anti-missile system. Although the two systems are only provided with one ton of ammunition to share, this is considered adequate in light of Atropos' other defensive systems.
