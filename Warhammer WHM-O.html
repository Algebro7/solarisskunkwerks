Capabilities
The Primary configuration, although not meeting the ancient Warhammer's "Total Combat" profile, is deadly against 'Mechs. Twin heavy PPCs deliver exceptional energy-on-target and an MML-9 provides flexible backup. Sixteen double heat sinks keep excessive heat at bay.

Configuration A Is used mainly for fire-support, especially in Davion forces. It tends to fight weak up close, but a strong battery of Streak SRM-6s help keep the foolish away. Two ER Small Lasers provide decent point-blank firepower.

Embracing the "Total Combat" philosophy to the fullest, B Config can fight equally well at all ranges and against all opponents. Utilizing the flexible Multiple Missile Launcher, as well as later model PPCs, this is one of the more favored configurations among Kuritan forces.

C Configuration boasts a complete departure from the Warhammer mould by redefining the heavy in-fighter role. Only useful during confined battles, such as in a city, C config uses two sets of lasers as layered firepower and supplements them with accurate, withering missile fire.
